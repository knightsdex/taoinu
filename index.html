Create a wallet

Download Metamask or your wallet of choice from the app store, google play store or as a chrome extension on desktop by going to metamask.io.

Get some ETH

The current trading pair requires you to have ETH in exchange for $TAONU. You additionally need it to cover the gas fees. It can be bought directly on metamask, other on-ramps, or on exchanges to send it to your wallet.

Swap ETH to TAONU

Go to swap.taoinu.com and paste the $TAONU token address into the receive-token field or use the provided link. Connect your wallet and swap ETH to TAONU. When Metamask prompts you for a wallet signature, sign.
